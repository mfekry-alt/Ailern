Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AiLern ecosystem.

How do I export system reports? expand_more
Reports can be exported directly from the dashboard header using the "Export Report" button. We support PDF, CSV, and Excel formats for your convenience.
Is there a limit to the number of instructors? expand_more
No, AiLern scales with your institution. You can add unlimited instructors and students depending on your subscription tier.
Can I customize the dashboard colors? expand_more
Yes, "System Settings" allows you to modify the branding, including the primary color and logo, to match your organization's identity.
